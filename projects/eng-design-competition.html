SFU Engineering Design Competition

Project Overview

Designed and built a lightweight structure in a 4-person team to transport materials across a 3–5 m gap under timed competition conditions.

Skills Used

Mechanical Design Rapid Prototyping Iterative Testing Teamwork Structural Design Problem Solving

How It Works

The challenge was to move materials across a 3–5 m gap as quickly as possible under timed conditions. The team designed a lightweight structure to do this, then used rapid prototyping and iterative testing to refine the design — adjusting it to meet the competition constraints while improving speed and reliability run after run.

Main Features

  • Lightweight structure to transport materials across a 3–5 m gap.
  • Designed and built in a 4-person team under timed conditions.
  • Rapid prototyping and iterative testing to refine the design.
  • Tuned the design to meet competition constraints and improve performance.
